## What this gate did — four generator changes, then re-render the 78 + 20
Small changes to the shared panel/compose, folded in and re-rendered. Step A (counts + design + one proof) was signed off (V2 filled-region haze; four confirmations; thin-n note on ≤5-px sites).

### (a) Site inset — red paddock highlight
`gayini_locator_inset` gained an optional `mark` (red site dot); `area` may now be `NULL`. Site maps highlight the site's **management zone in red** with a dot for the site (39 resolved sites); the **18 no-zone sites get the dot only** (no red zone). The site's zone is resolved fresh by centroid-in-zone (`gayini_resolve_site`), matching the D2 `plot_paddock` recon.

### (b) Retire the community×wetness legend on paddock dashboards
Removed the boxed 3×3 key from the **21 paddock** maps (it overlapped the map, e.g. Mara 21). The "Where it sits" boxplot carries the community-hue key. **Sites unaffected** (their map is the flood-frequency neighbourhood). **Flag (not this task):** the dropped low/mid/high *wetness-band* dimension must live on the whole-farm map at the front of the main report — its one home.

### (c) Site veg×water panel — NESTED paddock haze (V2), not swapped
The marker panel gained `haze_geom`. When a site has a paddock, its paddock's community pixels are drawn as a **translucent community-hue density region** (V2: `stat_density_2d` polygon, constant fill, alpha-stacked — no second fill scale) under the GAM line: **community (grey) → paddock (haze) → site (red ◆)**. Degrade cascade holds: **no paddock → no haze**; **no census px → note, no marker**. Paddocks themselves get **no haze** (the marker is the paddock; its own-cloud shows the full distribution).

### (d) Sparse-tail caption + thin-n note
Every veg×water panel now carries the sparse-tail line: *"Dashed line = sparse-tail boundary: GAM floor trend fit only to its left (≥500 community pixels per flood-freq bin)."* The **own-clouds** get the analogous *"Each community line is drawn only over its well-supported flood range (≥500 px/bin)"* (so the 20 were re-rendered). Sites whose marker rests on **≤5 census pixels** carry an extra line: *"Marker rests on only N census pixels — a very small footprint sample."*
